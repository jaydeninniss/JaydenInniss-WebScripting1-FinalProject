VIDEO REEL

This video was made for School District 48 in collaboration with Tanina Williams. It had the goal of creating a video of Tanina Williams teaching staff how to make Plank Drums and play a traditional Sk̲wx̲wú7mesh and Lil̓wat7úl game. This project was a success as this video is being used to educate students/staff and is playing a role in Truth and Reconciliation efforts within our school district.
I feel very fortunate to have been approached for this project as this video will continue to be used throughout the community for years to come.
